How to Measure

Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your chest, keeping the tape horizontal.

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, keeping the tape comfortably loose.

Length

Measure from the base of your neck to where you want the garment to end.

Shoulder

Measure from the edge of one shoulder to the edge of the other shoulder.

Size Tips

General Tips
  • Always measure over light clothing
  • Keep the measuring tape snug but not tight
  • Measure twice to ensure accuracy
  • Consider your preferred fit (slim, regular, loose)
Fit Guide
  • Slim Fit: Tighter, more fitted appearance
  • Regular Fit: Standard, comfortable fit
  • Loose Fit: Relaxed, roomy fit
  • When in doubt, size up for comfort
